who we are

Veloce is a boutique Technology Company focused in Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and IoT.

Veloce Technologies has a variety of applications for Virtual Reality (VR), Augment Reality (AR) and Internet of Things (IoT) for various industries including Entertainment / Theme Parks, Education, Real Estate, Shopping Malls, Tourism etc.

Entertainment

Virtual Reality is the next generation entertainment attraction which is all set to capture the entire entertainment industry and we have taken our first step in delivering world class Virtual Reality Simulated Rides. We are launching a variety of Virtual Reality Rides which will unquestionably take the audience to the next level of gaming experience. We have developed various VR experiences/games.

Education

Veloce education products in AR/VR technology provides the next generation learning experience which enable the student for deeper understanding of the subject. This digital interactive and engaging experience makes learning more pleasure.

Tourism

Tourism revenue constitutes of more that 10% of most of the country’s revenue. Yes there is always been a huge limitation in allowing the Tourism Department, Tour Operators and Consolidators to exhibit the uniqueness of a particular attraction, Veloce applications and products will provide an amazing platform for the Tourism Industry to uniquely market their attractions.

Entertainment Products

Bringing Fun to everyday lives!

Veloce has developed a technology platform which enables people to physically experience the happenings of a immersive video being played on a VR headset. This patent pending technology is used in various products like extreme rider, flight simulator, hot balloon rides etc. which has been installed in malls, entertainment centres across major cities in India.

Extreme Rider

Four patrons will be made to sit on the motion base mounted seats and will be given a Virtual Reality (VR) Headset. The VR Headset will play content similar to Roller Coaster and the seats will move in synchronization with the VR content thereby providing immersive experience.

Flying Simulator

One patron will be suspended in air through harness and will be mounted on to the motion platform and will be given a Virtual Reality (VR) Headset. The VR Headset will play content similar to Paragliding and the patron will move in synchronization with the VR content thereby will have any amazing flying experience.

Car Racing Simulator

One racer will be made to sit on a motion base platform designed like REAL CAR and will be given a Virtual Reality (VR) Headset. The racer can now start the vehicle and start racing in the track he desires to. The VR Headset content and the motion platform will move in synchronization thereby providing the racer with ultimate immersive racing experience.

The Shooter

One player will be made to stand on a special platform, supported with harness and made to stand inside a ring will be given a Virtual Reality (VR) Headset. The player can run, walk, jump and shoot the opponents as in real life. This will provide the player with highest gaming experience.

Education Products

Engaged Experience, Enhanced Understanding and Convenient Learning.

Veloce education products in AR/VR technology provides the next generation learning experience which enable the student for deeper understanding of the subject. This digital interactive and engaging experience makes learning more pleasure.

AR Book App

AR Book App is an Augmented Reality App which will bring life to the subjects in the book and will also allow the students to interact with the digital subjects. This App will also provide the students with the video of the subject explanation which will allow the students to learn and understand the subjects thoroughly at their convenience.

VR Lab

In Virtual Reality Lab the students of different classes will wear the VR Headsets and conduct various of their Physics, Chemistry and Biology experiments and will allow the students to master the experiments through immersive learning.

AR interactive Class Room

AR Interactive Classroom will be a unique classroom where the designated Animated Character will teach the students of various classes about different subjects including but not limited to Water Cycle, Evolution, Solar System, Human Anatomy Etc. Students can interact with the animated character in real-time and live and learn about the subjects in detail in digital format.

VR Pods

The VR Pods will allow the students to walk around in the Virtual Reality Environment and also interact with the Virtual Reality Objects in Real-Time. In the VR Pods the students will physically and virtually move around in the virtual reality environment by walking on the specially designed VR POD.
